Confidential Reporting System for Whistleblowers

The company has established a confidential reporting system that enables whistleblowers to confidentially report concerns relating to Product Safety, integrity, quality, and legality to senior management.

Purpose of the Confidential Reporting System

  • Discourage unethical behavior.
  • Increase the chances of detection.
  • Discourage fraudulent activity.

Additional Benefits of the System

  • Encourage use as a prevention tool.
  • Enhance existing quality tools.
  • Ensure product quality and safety.

Reporting Channels

  • Line Manager: Raise concerns with your immediate supervisor.
  • HR Department: Discuss issues with Human Resources.
  • Suggestion Box: Use the box in the canteen for anonymous reporting.

Areas of Concern

You are encouraged to report concerns relating to the following areas, or any other concerns you may have.
