PPE: Protecting Our Products and You

Proper Use

All PPE, except boots, must be kept off the floor. If contact occurs, discard and replace immediately. Never wear PPE without a hair covering.

Storage

Personal lockers are strictly for personal items. Do not store medication, food, or drinks. PPE is company-issued and must not be taken off-site.

Step 1: Hairnet

Put on your hairnet (and beard snood if applicable). You may need two hairnets for longer hair. This must always be the first step.

Step 2: Footwear

Put on your company-issued hat and boots or safety shoes.

Step 3: Sanitise Hands

Sanitise your hands thoroughly before touching your clean work coat.

Step 4: Coat

Put on your coat and fasten all the way up to cover your personal clothing completely.

Step 5: Final Hand Wash

After you are fully dressed, wash and disinfect your hands one last time before entering the production area.
