ü¶∫ Module 2: PPE & Protective Equipment

Personal Protective Equipment is your shield! Learn how to use it properly to protect yourself and our products.

üëî The Right Order - PPE Steps

  • Hairnet & Beard Snood: Put on your hairnet first (and beard snood if needed). You may need two hairnets for longer hair
  • Hat & Footwear: Put on your hat and appropriate boots/shoes
  • Hand Sanitising: Sanitise your hands thoroughly
  • Coat: Put on your coat and fasten all the way up to cover your personal clothing
  • Final Hand Wash: Wash and disinfect your hands again

üìù The Golden Rules

üö® Critical: All PPE (except boots) must be kept off the floor. If it touches the floor, discard and replace immediately!
