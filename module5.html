Caring for Our Environment

At Hilltop, protecting the environment is as important as producing great honey. Your actions, big and small, make a huge difference. This module covers the simple, common-sense ways you can help us be a green and responsible workplace.

Rule #1: Clean Up Spills Immediately

Why it Matters

Honey spills can attract pests and are a safety hazard. More importantly, honey washed down the drain can seriously harm local rivers and bee populations.

What to Do

Clean up any spills right away using the provided spill kits. **Never** wash honey or syrup down the drain. Use designated containers for disposal.

Rule #2: Recycle Correctly

Recycling only works when we do it right! Putting the wrong item in a bin can contaminate the whole batch. Please take a moment to sort your waste correctly.

  • Paper & Cardboard: In the paper recycling bin.
  • Plastic & Glass: In the mixed recycling bin. Ensure containers are washed first.
  • Food Waste: In the designated compost bin.

If you're unsure, ask your manager. It's better to ask than to guess!

Rule #3: See Something? Say Something.

You are our eyes and ears on the factory floor. If you see an environmental risk, we need to know.

What to Report

Report any leaks, large spills, improper waste disposal, or anything else that could harm the environment.

Who to Tell

Tell your team leader or manager immediately. Your quick action can prevent a small problem from becoming a big one.
