Factory Rules & Guidelines

Smoking & Vaping Policy

  • Designated Area Only: Smoking and vaping are permitted only in the designated smoking area.
  • No Indoor Smoking: Smoking and vaping inside the building are strictly prohibited.
  • Proper Disposal: Dispose of cigarette butts and vaping waste in provided bins.

Food Storage & Personal Belongings

Food Care

Take care of your own food.

No Lockers

Don't store food in your lockers.

Sealed Containers

Use sealed containers for storage in provided fridges.

Searches & Cleanliness

  • Search Policy: Management may request searches of clothing, baggage, lockers, or vehicles.
  • Witness Required: Searches must be conducted by same-sex management with an independent witness.
  • Clean As You Go: Always maintain a clean and tidy workspace.
  • Shared Facilities: Clean up after yourself when using shared facilities.

Equipment & Personal Responsibility

  • Authorisation Required: Company equipment (fax, phone, computer) requires permission.
  • Restricted Devices: No photographic or recording equipment in production areas.
  • Electrical Equipment: Keep secured in a locker.
  • Solely Responsible: You are responsible for your possessions on site.

Compliance & Consequences

Failure to comply with company standards may result in disciplinary action.

This includes, but is not limited to, improper handling of food contact packaging.

Always dispose of waste in designated bins.

Adherence to these rules is essential for maintaining safe food production practices.
