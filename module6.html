Food Handler Safety Agreement

This outlines the health reporting requirements and hygiene policies for food handlers. It covers reportable conditions, household illness, and compliance expectations.

Reportable Health Conditions

Enteric Fever

Includes Typhoid and Paratyphoid.

Other Infections

Salmonella, Shigella, VTEC E. coli (0157), Cholera.

Intestinal Illnesses

Diarrhea, vomiting, stomach and bowel disorders.

Reporting Requirements

  • Before Work: Report any listed conditions to your manager, team leader, or HR.
  • After Time Off: Report if any conditions occurred during your absence.
  • Household Illness: Report recurring diarrhea/vomiting in family members.

Medical Assessment

Doctor's Visit

You may be required to see a personal doctor for a fitness assessment.

Company Policy

You must understand and comply with all hygiene and food handling procedures.

Legal and Employment Requirements

Legal Obligation

Reporting health conditions is a legal requirement for food handlers.

Employment Condition

Compliance is a condition of your employment.

Consequences

Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action or dismissal.
