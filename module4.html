Hilltop Honey Ltd. - Security and Safety

Welcome to Hilltop Honey Ltd., where we prioritize the safety and security of our employees and visitors. This presentation outlines our comprehensive security measures and the crucial role everyone plays in maintaining a safe and productive environment.

Site Access and Security

Access Control

Unidentified visitors are strongly encouraged to be challenged, and factory management should be alerted immediately if any doubt exists. All doors and gates must be closed at all times.

Security Checks

Management may request to search employees' clothing, baggage, lockers, or vehicles. These searches will be conducted in the presence of an independent witness and by a member of the same sex.

Communication and Personal Devices

Company Systems

Using company's fax, telephone, or computer systems without authorized management approval is strictly prohibited.

Electrical Equipment

Electrical equipment should be left at home whenever possible. If not, it must be secured in a locker or left at reception.

Personal Belongings

Employees are solely responsible for the safety and security of their personal belongings while on site.

CCTV Surveillance

Purpose

To provide a safe environment, protect property, deter crime, and may be used in disciplinary procedures.

Compliance

The system operates in accordance with GDPR and other relevant legislation, meeting industry standards like BRC.

Privacy

The system is operated with respect for individual privacy and is in operation 24/7.

CCTV: Image Management and Disclosure

Disclosure

CCTV images will not be disclosed to third parties unless required by law enforcement (police) or for valid and documented reasons, such as insurance claims investigations.

Location and Access

CCTV cameras are strategically placed throughout the site, not always in obvious locations. Access to images is restricted to authorized staff.

CCTV: Data Retention and Requests

  • Retention: CCTV footage is retained for a maximum of 30 days unless required for an ongoing investigation.
  • Requests: Requests for access to CCTV material should be made in writing to Scott Davies at the Hilltop Honey Vastre address.

Factory Access and PPE

Restricted Areas

Do not enter areas of the factory without permission. These areas may have restricted access due to safety hazards or required PPE.

Questions

If you have any doubts about access or safety, please ask a manager or authorized personnel.

Reporting Concerns

Safety and Security Concerns

If you have any concerns about security or safety, please contact any manager as soon as possible. Your input is valuable in maintaining a secure workplace.

Safety and Security: Everyone's Responsibility

Shared Responsibility

Your safety and food safety are important to us, and everyone plays a role in maintaining a secure environment.

Collaboration

By working together, we can ensure a safe and productive workplace for everyone.
